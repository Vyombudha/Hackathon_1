About FoodShare

FoodShare is a platform dedicated to connecting generous donors with people in need through our network of restaurant partners. Our mission is to reduce food waste and hunger simultaneously by creating an efficient system for food donation and distribution.

Founded in 2023, we've already helped provide thousands of meals to people experiencing food insecurity while helping restaurants reduce waste and give back to their communities.
